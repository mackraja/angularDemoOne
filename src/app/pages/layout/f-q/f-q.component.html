Frequently asked questions

What is proxy access?

Each account requires a unique email address. However, family members sometimes share an email address. In this case, we offer what is known as “proxy” access. This allows each family member to sign up using the same username and password. They will have access to each other’s health information. Proxy access can be used between spouses, when an adult is the caretaker for another adult, or for parents to access their children’s accounts. If you'd like to set up a proxy account, you may request proxy access at your next hospital or clinic visit.
